Bottlenose Dolphin

They can use tools (sponging) and transmit cultural knowledge from generation to generation, and their considerable intelligence has driven interaction with humans.

  • Scientific Name: Tursiops truncatus
  • Average Length: 13 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 39 years
  • Habitat: Warm and temperate seas

The bottle-nose dolphin weighs an average of 660 pounds. It can reach a length of just over 13 feet. It's color is usually dark gray on the back and lighter gray on the flanks. Older dolphins sometimes have a few spots.
